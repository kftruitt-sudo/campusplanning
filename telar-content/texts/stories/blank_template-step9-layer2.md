As Dr. Radde emphasizes, the relationship between UCSB and the local Chumash tribes is built on respect, dignity, and trust. Rebuilding trust is a slow process — and rightfully so. After all, there have been centuries of entire Indigenous communities being annihilated. That's what the HSSB's repository is all about--it's a physical manifestation of NAGPRA's goals.
