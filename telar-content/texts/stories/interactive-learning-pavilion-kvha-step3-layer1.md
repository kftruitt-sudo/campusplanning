Nature-inspired design is core to the shape of the building as well. The monolithic, opposing forms of the terraced structures are another nod to the beach cliffs. There's also a core channel between them, providing more space for pedestrian circulation. Human-focused design is key here, as in all of LMN's other projects, since the founding of the studio in 1979.
