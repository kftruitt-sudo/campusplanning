As all other UCSB buildings, the ILP started as a goal in a [[lrdp-id|LRDP]]. In 2010, UCSB released another LRDP: the one that was used for the ILP's planning process. Among other recommendations, the [2010 LRDP](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1sv6C1r957kwLHIIXls57_3y4WP6aXRh_/view) states, "Views of the mountains and sea should be an integral part of the design of both indoor and outdoor spaces." This core tenet was not just kept in mind when the ILP was designed, but expanded upon in many ways.
