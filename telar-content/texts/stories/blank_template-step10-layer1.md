The [2020 amendment of CalNAGPRA](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201920200AB275) is key here, because it strengthened the rights of non-federally recognized tribes in terms of repatriation. Now, non-federally recognized tribes in California are able to initiate the repatriate process. That's one way that you can make some change: if enough people bring up the issue with their local lawmakers, Indigenous tribes in other states may also benefit from this type of amendment!
